## 1. The one-liner

**SellerBot is an AI sales agent that runs your Instagram DMs for you — it answers product questions, negotiates the price, collects and verifies payment, and does it all in your own Hinglish voice.**

Think of it as a tireless salesperson sitting in your Instagram inbox 24/7, closing deals while you sleep, and handing the conversation back to you the moment you want to step in.

---

## 2. Who it's for

Indian small-business sellers who run their storefront through **Instagram DMs** — the creators and shop owners selling watches, clocks, home décor, frames, apparel, and similar goods.

The pain we remove:
- DMs pile up; replies are slow; customers go cold.
- The same five questions ("price kya hai?", "warranty hai?", "delivery kitne din?") answered a hundred times a day.
- Haggling every single chat, manually, on a phone.
- No way to be online at 11pm when buyers actually message.

**One person can now handle the inbox of a whole team — without sounding like a robot.**

## 3. What it actually does (Shipping today)

### Sells in *your* voice, in Hinglish
Not a stiff English chatbot. SellerBot speaks natural Hindi-English the way Indian sellers actually talk — "Haan ji, kya chahiye?" — and you can tune the personality: greeting style, how firm it haggles, favourite phrases, emoji level, Hindi-to-English ratio. It even **detects the customer's likely gender from their name** and adjusts how it addresses them.

### Negotiates like a pro — and never loses you money
This is the headline feature. SellerBot runs a real, multi-round negotiation strategy:
- Holds firm at first, defends on quality before it ever discounts.
- Concedes gradually and intelligently — small counters for warm buyers, better offers for someone about to walk away.
- **Never goes below your floor price.** Ever. You set the lowest you'll accept; the bot protects it.
- **Never quotes higher than a price it already showed** — so it always looks consistent and trustworthy.
- Handles **bulk orders** ("5 chahiye") with automatic per-piece discounts, and can **bundle multiple products** into one deal without breaking your margins.

### Identifies products from photos *and* reels
Customer sends a picture of what they want? The bot recognises it and pulls up the matching product from your catalogue. Customer drops an **Instagram reel link**? It matches that too — then sends back the product photo and price automatically.

### Answers product questions — and knows when to ask you
"Warranty kitne ka hai?", "material kya hai?", "size?" — SellerBot answers from your product specs. And when a customer asks something you *haven't* filled in yet, it doesn't guess — it **quietly alerts you to add the detail**, so the bot is never caught making things up.

### Collects and verifies payment
Closes the loop end to end. It shares your **UPI / bank details**, receives the payment screenshot, **reads the screenshot to confirm it's a real payment** (not a random photo), and marks the order confirmed — or flags it to you if something's off.

### Hands the conversation to you instantly — and takes it back gracefully
The **human-in-the-loop** feature: the moment you type a reply yourself from your normal Instagram inbox, the bot detects it and **steps back automatically** so you're never talking over each other. After a quiet period it resumes — and while you were handling it, it kept reading along, so it picks up with full context.

### Reads the room
A real-time classifier tracks customer **sentiment and intent** on every message — spotting frustration, repeated complaints, or a buyer about to leave — and can **alert you to step in** before a deal goes sideways.

## 4. Why it's different

- **It negotiates — most "chatbots" just answer FAQs.** Price-floor protection and consistent quoting are the moat.
- **It sounds human and local.** Hinglish, persona-matched, gender-aware — not a translated Western bot.
- **It's safe by design.** Floor prices, no hallucinated specs, screenshot-verified payments, instant human handoff.
- **It's built for how Indian sellers actually sell** — UPI-first, COD-optional, Instagram-native, reel-aware.
